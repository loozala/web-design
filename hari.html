water pollution

water pollution is the contaminationof water sources by substances which make the water unusable for drinking, cooking, cleaning, swimming, and other activities. Pollutants include chemicals, trash, bacteria, and parasites. All forms of pollution eventually make their way to water.

    causes of water pollution
  • global warming
  • deforestation
  • Industry, agriculture and livestock farming.
  • Rubbish and faecal water dumping.
